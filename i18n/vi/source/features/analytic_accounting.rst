Analytic Accounting
===================

.. i18n: The analytic accounting is the core of all management modules of Open ERP.  It
.. i18n: is perfectly integrated with all operations that have a (direct or indirect) cost
.. i18n: or revenue. As an example, for services companies, the analytic accounting is

The analytic accounting is the core of all management modules of Open ERP.  It
is perfectly integrated with all operations that have a (direct or indirect) cost
or revenue. As an example, for services companies, the analytic accounting is
used as the financial part of project management.

.. i18n: As all tools are integrated, it gives you a full control and automatisation of
.. i18n: your company:

As all tools are integrated, it gives you a full control and automatisation of
your company:

.. i18n: * Plannings are integrated with budgets,
.. i18n: * Project management is integrated with contracts and affairs managmeent, etc.

* Plannings are integrated with budgets,
* Project management is integrated with contracts and affairs managmeent, etc.
