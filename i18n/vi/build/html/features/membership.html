Membership¶

The membership module allows associations to manage their members. From the search of new members to the periodical invoicing of current members. It’s linked to the portal module to allow you to provide online access to members on your system: calendar of events, current projects, members lists, etc.

Key Points:¶

  • Free members, fixed price, associated members, price management.
  • Automatic invoicing and propositions
  • Configurable workflow

Integration Benefits:¶

  • Integrated with accounting for membership invoicing
  • Integrated to pricelists to provide specific prices for members
  • Integrated with portal to provide access to members
