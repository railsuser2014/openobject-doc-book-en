#################
Open ERP Exercice
#################

.. i18n:  Open ERP is a wonderful application, having lots of different features in
.. i18n:  hundreds of functionnal area. The best way to learn Open ERP is to practice in
.. i18n:  a real situation.  This chapter present a learning exercise, based on a simple
.. i18n:  example, to gradually make you discover the basic functions of Open ERP.  The
.. i18n:  goal of this tutorial/excercice is a unified presentation of the main features
.. i18n:  of the software, without considering the various details and parameters.

 Open ERP is a wonderful application, having lots of different features in
 hundreds of functionnal area. The best way to learn Open ERP is to practice in
 a real situation.  This chapter present a learning exercise, based on a simple
 example, to gradually make you discover the basic functions of Open ERP.  The
 goal of this tutorial/excercice is a unified presentation of the main features
 of the software, without considering the various details and parameters.

.. i18n:  Based on a real case, this chapter covers: installation and setup, configuration
.. i18n:  of the database, sales management, customer relationship management, project
.. i18n:  and services, accounting, logistic and manufacturing.

 Based on a real case, this chapter covers: installation and setup, configuration
 of the database, sales management, customer relationship management, project
 and services, accounting, logistic and manufacturing.
