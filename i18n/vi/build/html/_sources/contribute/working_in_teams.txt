.. i18n: Quality team are people from Tiny sprl responsible for the quality of the
.. i18n: Official repository. They assume the stability and coherence of the Official
.. i18n: version by review the community patch and commit.

Quality team are people from Tiny sprl responsible for the quality of the
Official repository. They assume the stability and coherence of the Official
version by review the community patch and commit.

.. i18n: They're in charge to merge the commit from Community repository to Official
.. i18n: repository. "Extra_addons" and "Others" repository are not part of their
.. i18n: responsability.

They're in charge to merge the commit from Community repository to Official
repository. "Extra_addons" and "Others" repository are not part of their
responsability.

.. i18n: If a commit isn't good enough, they must remove it from Community repository
.. i18n: and report it in the :ref:`bug tracker <bug-tracker-link>`. In other case, they
.. i18n: will apply the purposed commit in the Official version. Like this, everyone
.. i18n: will stay aware about what is or isn't accepted.

If a commit isn't good enough, they must remove it from Community repository
and report it in the :ref:`bug tracker <bug-tracker-link>`. In other case, they
will apply the purposed commit in the Official version. Like this, everyone
will stay aware about what is or isn't accepted.

.. i18n: Introduction
.. i18n: """"""""""""

Introduction
""""""""""""

.. i18n: As to help developers and contributors to take the right decision when
.. i18n: improving OpenERP, we set up experts teams for different management domains.
.. i18n: Only people that have a strong experience in OpenERP and the related domain can
.. i18n: apply as an expert. We have teams of accountants, manufacturing experts,
.. i18n: technical experts, services management experts, ...

As to help developers and contributors to take the right decision when
improving OpenERP, we set up experts teams for different management domains.
Only people that have a strong experience in OpenERP and the related domain can
apply as an expert. We have teams of accountants, manufacturing experts,
technical experts, services management experts, ...

.. i18n: Developers can contact our experts mailing list when they need feedback on
.. i18n: particular features to be developed. Please contact our experts only for new
.. i18n: developments related questions. They don't provide help on current features of
.. i18n: OpenERP. Most of our experts have very high positions in the company they work
.. i18n: for, so they don't have time to spent providing help or support.

Developers can contact our experts mailing list when they need feedback on
particular features to be developed. Please contact our experts only for new
developments related questions. They don't provide help on current features of
OpenERP. Most of our experts have very high positions in the company they work
for, so they don't have time to spent providing help or support.

.. i18n: Requesting Advices to A Team
.. i18n: ^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Requesting Advices to A Team
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. i18n: When you `create a specification <https://blueprints.launchpad.net/openobject/+addspec>`_ 
.. i18n: of a new feature on launchpad (called a blueprint), you can
.. i18n: assign an expert team as a drafter of the specifications. Then, you can click
.. i18n: on request feedback on your blueprint and assign this to an expert team.

When you `create a specification <https://blueprints.launchpad.net/openobject/+addspec>`_ 
of a new feature on launchpad (called a blueprint), you can
assign an expert team as a drafter of the specifications. Then, you can click
on request feedback on your blueprint and assign this to an expert team.

.. i18n: They will receive a notification email and will discuss about the requested
.. i18n: feature. The team will improve your specifications directly in your blueprint.

They will receive a notification email and will discuss about the requested
feature. The team will improve your specifications directly in your blueprint.
