********************************
Customer Relationship Management
********************************

.. i18n:  *It's often said that the customer is king. You would treat all your customers as royalty, 
.. i18n:  at the center of your attention if you had a way of keeping your eyes on them at all times.
.. i18n:  Open ERP's CRM module is designed to make this aim a reality, helping employees of the business
.. i18n:  understand their customers' needs better, and automating their communication efforts.*

 *It's often said that the customer is king. You would treat all your customers as royalty, 
 at the center of your attention if you had a way of keeping your eyes on them at all times.
 Open ERP's CRM module is designed to make this aim a reality, helping employees of the business
 understand their customers' needs better, and automating their communication efforts.*

.. tip:: CRM & SRM

   CRM is the abbreviation for Customer Relationship Management, and SRM is Supplier Relationship
   Management.

.. i18n: If you want to focus on your customers, you need tools to make that focus easy. Tools that will
.. i18n: capture all the knowledge you have available, tools that will help you analyze what you know, and
.. i18n: tools that will make it easy to use all of that knowledge and analysis.

If you want to focus on your customers, you need tools to make that focus easy. Tools that will
capture all the knowledge you have available, tools that will help you analyze what you know, and
tools that will make it easy to use all of that knowledge and analysis.

.. i18n: A crucial advantage that Open ERP gives you over the more specialist CRM applications is that
.. i18n: Open ERP knows more about your customers and your ability to supply them because it's handling all
.. i18n: of your accounting, sales, purchases, manufacturing and fulfilment as well as linking to all of
.. i18n: your internal staff.

A crucial advantage that Open ERP gives you over the more specialist CRM applications is that
Open ERP knows more about your customers and your ability to supply them because it's handling all
of your accounting, sales, purchases, manufacturing and fulfilment as well as linking to all of
your internal staff.

.. i18n: Open ERP's :mod:`crm` module uses that information and offers several significant features that enable
.. i18n: you and your staff to monitor and control your supplier and customer relationships effectively, such
.. i18n: as delegating issues to the most appropriate people, keeping a history of communications and events,
.. i18n: qualifying prospects and detecting problems.

Open ERP's :mod:`crm` module uses that information and offers several significant features that enable
you and your staff to monitor and control your supplier and customer relationships effectively, such
as delegating issues to the most appropriate people, keeping a history of communications and events,
qualifying prospects and detecting problems.

.. i18n: It also uses several statistical tools that can analyze relationships quantitatively – your
.. i18n: customer service performance and the quality of your suppliers, for example. Using performance
.. i18n: analysis, you can easily put a policy of real continuous improvement in place by developing an
.. i18n: automatic rules-based system in Open ERP.

It also uses several statistical tools that can analyze relationships quantitatively – your
customer service performance and the quality of your suppliers, for example. Using performance
analysis, you can easily put a policy of real continuous improvement in place by developing an
automatic rules-based system in Open ERP.

.. i18n: To minimize re-typing work, Open ERP provides an email gateway that links your emails to the
.. i18n: databases. This is a significant feature – many of your staff will then use Open ERP
.. i18n: automatically through email without ever logging into it themselves and having to learn a new
.. i18n: system.

To minimize re-typing work, Open ERP provides an email gateway that links your emails to the
databases. This is a significant feature – many of your staff will then use Open ERP
automatically through email without ever logging into it themselves and having to learn a new
system.

.. i18n: Finally, at the end of this chapter you'll see an efficient method of qualifying prospects or
.. i18n: customers that enables you to offer a service tailored to the potential value of different
.. i18n: prospects.

Finally, at the end of this chapter you'll see an efficient method of qualifying prospects or
customers that enables you to offer a service tailored to the potential value of different
prospects.

.. i18n: For this chapter you should start with a fresh database that includes demo data,
.. i18n: using the :guilabel:`CRM profile` and no particular chart of accounts configured. 

For this chapter you should start with a fresh database that includes demo data,
using the :guilabel:`CRM profile` and no particular chart of accounts configured.
