Cube Browser
============

.. i18n: The Business Intelligence cube browser allows end-users to easily navigate
.. i18n: through the informations and statistics of the company and design their customized
.. i18n: reports in a few drag & drop. You can consolidate, drill up, drill down, slice,
.. i18n: filter data to benefit from a very user-friendly report interface.

The Business Intelligence cube browser allows end-users to easily navigate
through the informations and statistics of the company and design their customized
reports in a few drag & drop. You can consolidate, drill up, drill down, slice,
filter data to benefit from a very user-friendly report interface.

.. i18n: The power of Business Intelligence becomes available to all users of the
.. i18n: system to extract the exact informations needed from any screen. It is also
.. i18n: able to analyse others databases, to allow you to consolidate data in one
.. i18n: single user interface.

The power of Business Intelligence becomes available to all users of the
system to extract the exact informations needed from any screen. It is also
able to analyse others databases, to allow you to consolidate data in one
single user interface.
