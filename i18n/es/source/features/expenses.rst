Expenses
========

.. i18n: This module allows you to manage your company's daily expenses. It gives you
.. i18n: access to your employees's notes fees and give you the right to complete and
.. i18n: validate the notes of a period or to refuse them. After validation it creates

This module allows you to manage your company's daily expenses. It gives you
access to your employees's notes fees and give you the right to complete and
validate the notes of a period or to refuse them. After validation it creates
an invoice for the employee.

.. i18n: Employee can encode their own expenses and the validation flow puts it
.. i18n: automatically in the accounting after validation of managers.

Employee can encode their own expenses and the validation flow puts it
automatically in the accounting after validation of managers.
