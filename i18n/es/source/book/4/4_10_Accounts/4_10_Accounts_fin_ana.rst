
.. i18n: Financial Analysis

Financial Analysis
==================

.. i18n: Various reports designed for financial analysis are based on the analytic accounts. Most of those
.. i18n: are available directly from the tree of accounts or from the form view of the account.

Various reports designed for financial analysis are based on the analytic accounts. Most of those
are available directly from the tree of accounts or from the form view of the account.

The cost ledger (quantities only)
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. i18n: This report gives the detail of entries for an analytic account and a list of selected journals.
.. i18n: Only quantities are reported for this analysis, not costs and revenues.

This report gives the detail of entries for an analytic account and a list of selected journals.
Only quantities are reported for this analysis, not costs and revenues.

.. i18n: The report is often used to print the number of hours worked on a project, without exposing the
.. i18n: costs and revenues. So you can show it to a client as a record of the hours worked on a particular
.. i18n: project.

The report is often used to print the number of hours worked on a project, without exposing the
costs and revenues. So you can show it to a client as a record of the hours worked on a particular
project.

.. i18n: To restrict the report to hours worked, without including sales and purchases, select only the
.. i18n: services journal for printing.

To restrict the report to hours worked, without including sales and purchases, select only the
services journal for printing.

.. i18n: .. tip:: Multiple printing
.. i18n: 
.. i18n: 	To print several analytic accounts at once you can make a multiple selection on the different
.. i18n: 	accounts in the tree of accounts.
.. i18n: 	Then click on the appropriate :guilabel:`Report` in the toolbar (in the web client), or 
.. i18n: 	select one of the :guilabel:`Print` reports (in the GTK client), to export the whole selection into a
.. i18n: 	single PDF document.

.. tip:: Multiple printing

	To print several analytic accounts at once you can make a multiple selection on the different
	accounts in the tree of accounts.
	Then click on the appropriate :guilabel:`Report` in the toolbar (in the web client), or 
	select one of the :guilabel:`Print` reports (in the GTK client), to export the whole selection into a
	single PDF document.

.. i18n: Inverted Analytic Balance

Inverted Analytic Balance
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. i18n: The inverted analytic balance provides a summary report relating general accounts and 
.. i18n: analytic accounts. This report shows the balances of the general accounts
.. i18n: broken down by the selected analytic accounts for a selected period.

The inverted analytic balance provides a summary report relating general accounts and 
analytic accounts. This report shows the balances of the general accounts
broken down by the selected analytic accounts for a selected period.

.. i18n: .. figure::  images/analytic_balance_inverse.png
.. i18n:    :scale: 50
.. i18n:    :align: center
.. i18n: 
.. i18n:    *The inverted analytic balance shows a breakdown of operations by analytic account (project)*

.. figure::  images/analytic_balance_inverse.png
   :scale: 50
   :align: center

   *The inverted analytic balance shows a breakdown of operations by analytic account (project)*

.. i18n: This enables you to analyze your costs by general account. For example, if you examine your general
.. i18n: account for staff salaries you can obtain all your salary costs broken down by the different
.. i18n: analytic (or project) accounts.

This enables you to analyze your costs by general account. For example, if you examine your general
account for staff salaries you can obtain all your salary costs broken down by the different
analytic (or project) accounts.

.. i18n: .. tip::  Multi-company
.. i18n: 
.. i18n: 	In a multi-company environment each company can have its own general chart of accounts on the same
.. i18n: 	database.
.. i18n: 	The two general charts of accounts are independent but can be linked in a third chart using a
.. i18n: 	view account to do the consolidation.
.. i18n: 
.. i18n: 	If the different companies collaborate on joint projects they may all share the same analytic chart
.. i18n: 	of accounts.
.. i18n: 	In this environment, the cross-related reports like the balance and inverted balance are extremely
.. i18n: 	useful because
.. i18n: 	they enable you to make an analysis per company by linking up to the general accounts.

.. tip::  Multi-company

	In a multi-company environment each company can have its own general chart of accounts on the same
	database.
	The two general charts of accounts are independent but can be linked in a third chart using a
	view account to do the consolidation.

	If the different companies collaborate on joint projects they may all share the same analytic chart
	of accounts.
	In this environment, the cross-related reports like the balance and inverted balance are extremely
	useful because
	they enable you to make an analysis per company by linking up to the general accounts.

.. i18n: The real revenue is given by the amount invoiced to the client. The theoretical revenue is given by
.. i18n: the sale price of different project costs which could be invoiced to the client. These give
.. i18n: different margin figures.

The real revenue is given by the amount invoiced to the client. The theoretical revenue is given by
the sale price of different project costs which could be invoiced to the client. These give
different margin figures.

.. i18n: For example, in the case of a fixed price project contract, the real sale price at the end of the
.. i18n: project will be equal to the contract negotiated with the client. The theoretical price gives the
.. i18n: amount that would have been invoiced if you had charged for all the time worked.

For example, in the case of a fixed price project contract, the real sale price at the end of the
project will be equal to the contract negotiated with the client. The theoretical price gives the
amount that would have been invoiced if you had charged for all the time worked.

.. i18n: :mod:`account_analytic_analysis` module creates new menus in the Project management module in
.. i18n: :menuselection:`Project Management --> Financial Project Management --> Analytic Accounts`.

To give project managers a direct view of their different projects, the 
:mod:`account_analytic_analysis` module creates new menus in the Project management module in
:menuselection:`Project Management --> Financial Project Management --> Analytic Accounts`.

.. i18n: These different menus give quick views that are useful for live projects. For each project you
.. i18n: can check if there are uninvoiced services, see the last invoice date and the last uninvoiced
.. i18n: service date, and get reports on the amounts received and those planned. So project managers have
.. i18n: all the information necessary to manage their project, shown in a single page.

These different menus give quick views that are useful for live projects. For each project you
can check if there are uninvoiced services, see the last invoice date and the last uninvoiced
service date, and get reports on the amounts received and those planned. So project managers have
all the information necessary to manage their project, shown in a single page.

In the following chapters you'll see how project managers can use this information to carry out
